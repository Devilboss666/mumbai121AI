Small companies and startups can offer unique advantages for PwBD professionals:

Inclusive culture – Many are flexible and open to creating accommodating roles

Individual attention – Easier to build direct relationships with team leaders

Faster growth – Skills and efforts are quickly recognized

Customized roles – Possibilities of tailoring work to your strengths

Broader learning – Chance to explore multiple skills and functions
