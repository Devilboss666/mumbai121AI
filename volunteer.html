Volunteer With Us

Be the Change. Make a Difference. Gain Experience.

Mumbai121 is a not-for-profit initiative that connects fresh talent and persons with benchmark disabilities (PwBD) to startups and small businesses in Mumbai. Our platform is 100% free for both candidates and companies — and it runs with the help of dedicated volunteers like you.

Are you passionate about making a real impact in people's lives? Do you believe that freshers and PwBD deserve equal access to paid job opportunities? Join us and become part of this purpose-driven movement!

Join as Volunteer

Volunteer Roles Available

Jobseeker & Employer Outreach

Connect with freshers, PwBD candidates and employers to create meaningful opportunities.

WhatsApp & Email Coordination

Handle day-to-day communication to keep candidates and companies connected.

Data Entry and Management

Maintain and update candidate and company records using Google Sheets.

Social Media Support

Help spread the word about Mumbai121 across platforms to reach more job seekers.

Website Assistance and Tech Support

Assist with website maintenance and provide technical support to the team.

Mentoring Freshers & PwBD

Guide freshers and PwBD candidates to become industry-ready and interview-confident.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Freshers and PwBD job seekers often go unnoticed, even as startups and small businesses face a growing demand for talent. We're changing that with a 100% free platform that opens doors to real opportunities and gives them the right break to launch their careers.

By volunteering with us, you'll be shaping a fairer job market that empowers individuals and strengthens startups — all while building your own skills, network and experience.

✅ What You'll Gain

Real Work Experience in recruitment, operations, outreach, marketing, or tech

Mentorship from senior professionals

Certificate of Contribution after your volunteering period

Recognition on our platform for outstanding contributors

Personal Satisfaction of helping others start their careers

Who Can Volunteer?

We welcome:

Experienced or Freshers — preferably with basic computer knowledge

Freshers looking for real experience

Experienced HR and social work professionals wanting to give back

PwBD champions and inclusion advocates

Anyone with time, discipline, and a commitment to honest work
